# Data-Analysis---Blinkit
This project was created as part of my data analytics portfolio to demonstrate skills in data visualization, financial analysis, and storytelling through Power Bi dashboards.
This project focuses on an end-to-end data analysis of Blinkit to evaluate sales performance, customer satisfaction, and inventory distribution.
Using Power BI, the project transforms raw data into interactive dashboards and KPI-driven insights to support data-driven decision-making.

The objective is to identify trends, inefficiencies, and optimization opportunities across products, outlets, and customer behavior, enabling better operational and strategic planning.
# Business Requirement
To perform an end-to-end analysis of Blinkit’s sales performance, customer satisfaction, and inventory distribution, leveraging KPIs and Power BI visualizations to uncover actionable insights and optimization opportunities.

# KPI’s Requirements
1. Total Sales: The overall revenue generated from all items sold.
2. Average Sales: The average revenue per sale.
3. Number of Items: The total count of different items sold.
4. Average Rating: The average customer rating for items sold.

# Chart’s Requirements

1. Total Sales by Fat Content:
	- Objective: Analyze the impact of fat content on total sales.
	- Additional KPI Metrics: Assess how other KPIs (Average Sales, Number of Items, Average Rating) vary with fat content.
	- Chart Type: Donut Chart.
2. Total Sales by Item Type:
	- Objective: Identify the performance of different item types in terms of total sales.
	- Additional KPI Metrics: Assess how other KPIs (Average Sales, Number of Items, Average Rating) vary with fat content.
	- Chart Type: Bar Chart.
3. Fat Content by Outlet for Total Sales:
	- Objective: Compare total sales across different outlets segmented by fat content.
	- Additional KPI Metrics: Assess how other KPIs (Average Sales, Number of Items, Average Rating) vary with fat content.
	- Chart Type: Stacked Column Chart.
4. Total Sales by Outlet Establishment:
	- Objective: Evaluate how the age or type of outlet establishment influences total sales.
	- Chart Type: Line Chart.
5. Sales by Outlet Size:
   - Objective: Analyze the correlation between outlet size and total sales.
	- Chart Type: Donut/ Pie Chart.
6. Sales by Outlet Location:
	- Objective: Assess the geographic distribution of sales across different locations.
	- Chart Type: Funnel Map.
7. All Metrics by Outlet Type:
	- Objective: Provide a comprehensive view of all key metrics (Total Sales, Average Sales, Number of Items, Average Rating) 	broken down by different outlet types.
	- Chart Type: Matrix Card.

# Dashboard Analysis & Conclusion
This dashboard provides a comprehensive analysis of Blinkit sales, customer satisfaction, and inventory distribution using key KPIs and interactive Power BI visualizations.

## Sales Insights:
- Low-fat items generate a higher share of total sales, indicating a strong customer preference toward healthier product options.
- A limited number of item categories contribute the majority of revenue, highlighting key focus areas for business growth.
- Medium and large outlets outperform small outlets in terms of total sales, suggesting better product availability and customer reach.
## Customer Satisfaction Insights
- Customer ratings remain largely consistent across item types, reflecting stable product quality.
- Products with higher sales volumes also maintain strong average ratings, indicating alignment between demand and customer satisfaction.

## Inventory & Distribution Insights
- Certain items show high visibility but low sales, suggesting overstocking or ineffective product placement.
- Fast-moving products in some outlets face inventory constraints, leading to potential revenue loss.
- Inventory distribution varies across outlet sizes, emphasizing the need for demand-based stock optimization.
## KPI-Level Observations
- Total Sales are concentrated within specific item categories and outlet sizes.
- Average Sales per Item helps identify premium or high-margin products.
- Number of Items vs Sales comparison reveals underperforming SKUs.
- Average Rating acts as a strong indicator of long-term product sustainability.

## Business Recommendations
- Increase inventory allocation for high-demand, high-rated products.
- Re-evaluate pricing or placement for low-selling but highly visible items.
- Focus marketing and promotions on top-performing item categories.
- Optimize product mix at smaller outlets to improve sales efficiency.

# Business Impact
Enables data-driven decisions for inventory optimization, sales strategy enhancement, and customer satisfaction improvement.

Helps stakeholders quickly identify top-performing products and operational gaps through interactive dashboards.
